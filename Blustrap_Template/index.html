Your resource center for your clubs.


Activity List

As a college student you will want to spend time having fun with friends and enjoying you college experience and as such you may want to go to clubs or activities this link will take you to a list of all current activities and clubs as well as any information you may want or need on them.


Activity Rules

This will provide you with a comprehensive list of the rules for any activities or clubs you might participate in. Please read them before joining any PTC Club.


Track your meetings!

Keeping a schedule up and running is important for a club to function. The core of all clubs is their meetings if you don't know when to meet then you dont really have a club. Use this meeting tracker to help organize and remind yourself of when your club's meetings may occur.


Fundraiser Requests

Here you will be able to fill out a form for your deisred fundraiser. Fundraising is a key element to making a club more successful. If you and your club would like to start one of these fundraisers then please fill out the form.


Check Requests

Here you can make requests for money orders from the school for any outstanding costs that the school is willing to support.
